Garlic Salmon

"This is one of my favorite recipes for when folks come for dinner. I've already shared this popular recipe with many friends. The best thing is it's " so easy "to make!"

  • :marker "1/4 c. dry sherry"
  • :marker "2 T. soy sauce"
  • :marker "2 T. vegetable oil"
  • :marker "2 T. dry mustard"
  • :marker "3-4 T. freshly minced garlic"
  • :marker "1 to 2 lbs salmon filets"
  1. :marker "Mix together first 5 ingredients in a shallow dish. Place the cleaned and de- boned salmon filets faced down in the mixture and allow to marinate for 1/2 to 1 hour. (Tip, I cut a large piece of salmon into the serving sizes as well.)"
  2. :marker "Preheat the broiler. Transfer filets to a baknig sheet, skin-down. Broil the salmon on the top rack for 7 to 9 minutes, or until done to your liking (I like it still pink in the center)."
  3. :marker "I usually serve this with an aromatic rice and a lightly seasoned stir fry."
